Our Story

Founded in 2006, charity: water set a bold mission to end the global water crisis. By partnering with local organizations and individuals, we've funded over 186,000 water projects across 29 countries, delivering clean water to more than 20.3 million people.

FAQ

Where does my money go?

100% of public donations goes directly to funding clean water projects. Private donors cover the costs of operating charity: water so money can continue funding future projects.

How do I know this is legit?

charity: water is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that also prioritizes transparency by providing photos that you helped fund, while keeping our full accounting book open to the public to review any audits and final reports.

How can I help?

Every dollar no matter how big or small, monthly or one time, will go towards our projects. You can become a one time donator, sponsor or brand partner.
